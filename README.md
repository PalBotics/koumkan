# Κούμ Καν (Koum Kan) Score Tracker

A mobile-friendly score tracker for the Greek card game Koum Kan, designed to be installed as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on iPhone and Android.

## Features

- Track scores for 2 or more players across multiple rounds
- Automatic bust detection — prompts a player when they hit 100+ points
- Buy-in system with doubling re-entry costs that grow the pot
- Players who buy back in reset to the highest non-busted score
- Live leaderboard with standings after every round
- Pot tracking with manual edit option
- Full round history with buy-in and elimination log
- Undo last round support
- Auto-saves progress so closing the browser mid-game is safe

## Installing on iPhone

1. Open the [live app](https://palbotics.github.io/koumkan) in **Safari**
2. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow pointing up)
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add** — the app will appear on your home screen like a native app

## Installing on Android

1. Open the [live app](https://palbotics.github.io/koumkan) in **Chrome**
2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right
3. Tap **Add to Home screen**

## How to Play

Each player pays the buy-in amount to start. The pot grows throughout the game. Points accumulate each round — lowest score wins. When a player reaches 100 or more points they must either:

- **Buy in** — pay double their last buy-in (added to the pot), and their score resets to match the highest active player's score
- **Leave** — exit the game

The last player remaining wins the pot.
